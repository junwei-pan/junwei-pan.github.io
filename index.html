Junwei Pan

I am currently a machine learning researcher at Tencent, focusing on recommendation systems, computational advertising and multi-task learning. Proir to that, I was a Principal Research Engineer at Yahoo! Labs/Research, working on streaming news recommendation, relevance search, and online advertising. I received my M.S. degree from Renmin University of China, advised by Prof. Jiaheng Lu.
